What do we offer?

Statistics

We offer consulting services and solutions in computational and statistical data analysis across various disciplines ranging from natural sciences to finance. Our main focus is represented by our experience in biomedical projects.

  • Successful quantitative analysis and modelling starts already with a sound data basis. We can assist you from begin on and give you advice on experimental design and data acquisition.
  • We support you in the interpretation of results. We help you finding correct and meanigful answers to questions that are relevant for you.
  • Benefit from our inter-disciplinary knowledge and broad expertise beyond statistics.
  • We can help you visualize and aggregate your results such that they become understandable and valuable for experts as well as non-experts.
