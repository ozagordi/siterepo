Who we are

The company

enGene Statistics GmbH has emerged as an ETH Spin-off from the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering, ETH Zurich. We are a young, innovative start-up company providing statistical consulting services across various diciplines for clients ranging from academia to industry. The company is 100% independent, i.e. fully owned and operated by its five executive partners.

The Team

Our team comprises highly qualified staff with a rare blend of skills. The backgrounds of our experts include biotechnology, computational and theoretical biology, and theoretical physics. During their scientific careers our staff members have all been involved in biologically inspired research and gained profound expertise in computational handling and statistical analysis of diverse genomics data.

 

Osvaldo Zagordi

Data scientist with substantive expertise in biology, Osvaldo obtained his master degree (laurea) in physics at Università di Napoli Federico II, and holds a PhD in statistical and biological physics from SISSA/ISAS in Trieste. Postdoc in computational biology at the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering of ETH Zurich and now scientific collaborator at the Institute of medical virology of University of Zurich. He has authored 20 scientific publications and is developer of several scientific software, among which is shorah, a software suite for the analysis of genetically heterogeneous populations.

 

Cem Sievers

Cem, a biotechnologist by training, obtained his Diploma from Technische Universität Braunschweig. He holds a PhD from ETH Zurich, where he worked in the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering. He is currently postdoctoral fellow at Harvard Medical School.

 

Federico Comoglio

Federico studied biotechnology at Università Amedeo Avogadro in Novara, Italy, and is now completing his PhD at ETH Zurich in the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering.

 

Moritz Gerstung

Moritz obtained his Diplom in physics at the university of Freiburg im Breisgau, and then a PhD in computational biology at ETH Zurich where he worked at the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering. He is currently a postdoctoral fellow at Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute in Cambridge, UK.

 

Conradin Kraemer

Conradin obtained both his diploma and PhD in physics at ETH Zurich. He then worked as a postdoc in Computational Biology at the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering in Basel. Beside his role as CFO for enGene Statistics GmbH he works currently in the financial industry.
